About Me

My name is Ted O'Connor. I'm an accomplished technical executive and software engineer with over 20 years of experience with highly available enterprise solutions in regulated industries. I've spent significant time in consulting, engineering and DevOps roles while working with all sorts of clients.

During my time at Wingspan Technology we took the company from six people to almost 50 before being acquired. While I haven't jumped around between many companies, I've worn many hats throughout my career. My passion has always been being that go-to person for the problems that no one else could fix. I've done that both as a software developer, a SaaS architect and a team leader.

After 19 years with the same company and a successful exit I am taking some time off while I try to find my next challenge. I'm looking to leverage my proven technical, management, and communication skills in a hands-on role at a smaller company. Feel free to reach out if you think we'd be a good match.
